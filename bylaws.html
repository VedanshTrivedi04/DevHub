DevHub Bylaws

The foundational rules and regulations governing DevHub - Coding Club PIEMR. These bylaws ensure transparency, proper functioning, and a thriving community dedicated to web development excellence.

Governance
Membership
Code of Conduct
I

Article I: Name and Purpose

1Name

The organization is named "Devhub."

2Purpose

Devhub fosters excellence in web development, collaboration, and innovation through interactive events, projects, and community building.

II

Article II: Membership

1Eligibility

Membership is open to all PIEMR students interested in web development.

2Rights and Responsibilities

Members can attend events, contribute to projects, and must uphold values like respect, collaboration, and ethics.

III

Article III: Governance and Leadership

1Executive Committee

Includes roles like President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Event Coordinator, and Technical Lead, each with defined duties.

IV

Article IV: Meetings and Events

1Meetings

Regular meetings are conducted at least biweekly during the semester.

2Events

Activities include workshops, hackathons, expert talks, and collaborative projects to enhance learning and teamwork.

3Event Participation

Members are encouraged to actively engage in organizing and attending events.

V

Article V: Code of Conduct

1Respect and Collaboration

Members must maintain respect and foster teamwork within the club.

2Ethical Behaviour

Members should adhere to ethical practices and avoid plagiarism.

3Commitment

Members are expected to actively participate in meetings, events, and projects to the best of their ability.

Important Note

These bylaws are designed to ensure fair governance and a positive community environment. All members are expected to familiarize themselves with these rules and uphold them in spirit and practice. For any clarifications or concerns regarding these bylaws, please contact the Executive Committee.
